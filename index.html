02 — About

Soft surface. Brutal signal.

WENG builds hard techno from contradiction: classical discipline against warehouse force, elegant stillness against high-BPM impact, a daytime career in AI against a nightlife built from sweat, pressure, and distortion.

Her sound is precise without becoming polite. It carries the restraint of someone who understands structure and the appetite of someone who would rather break it open at the right moment.

The result is a world of black ink on paper: minimal at first glance, volatile at the edges, and louder than it looks.
